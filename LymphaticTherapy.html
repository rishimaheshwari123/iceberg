What is Dermashape Lymphatic Treatment?

Dermashape Lymphatic Treatment is an advanced therapy that combines the principles of lymphatic drainage massage with cutting-edge technology. The treatment uses gentle, rhythmic movements and specialized tools to stimulate the lymphatic system, encouraging the removal of toxins and excess fluids from the body. This process not only enhances detoxification but also helps in reducing the appearance of cellulite and improving skin texture.


How Does Dermashape Lymphatic Treatment Work? The Dermashape Lymphatic Treatment works by using a combination of manual massage techniques and mechanical stimulation to promote lymphatic drainage. The treatment involves the use of a specialized device that applies gentle pressure and rhythmic movements to the skin, stimulating the lymphatic vessels and encouraging the flow of lymph fluid.
